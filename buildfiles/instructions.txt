In Eclipse, One the build.xml file

Open the outline panel on the right

It will list all the targets with green bull's eyes.  

Control-click on the target "Build All" you want to build and a pop up will appear with 'run as', 'build as', 'debug as'.  
Mouse over 'run as' and a second menu will appear.  

choose the first  'Ant build' in the menu

Then look in the Targets folder for the results
